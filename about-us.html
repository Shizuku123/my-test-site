About Us

Our Story

With a strong track record spanning over 15 years, Tahanan ng Pagmamahal Children's Home, Inc. (TNPCHI) was established in July 2009 as a non-stock, non-profit organization. The organization is driven by their deep concern for our society's abandoned, neglected, foundling, and surrendered children, the couple and their dedicated friends embarked on a remarkable journey despite financial constraints.

In the face of economic challenges, they initiated their mission by welcoming the children's development and arranging temporary housing through rented apartments. Their unwavering commitment and resourcefulness empowered them to create a significant and positive difference in the lives of these vulnerable children.

In 2014, a generous supporter generously donated the land where Tahanan stands today, offering a permanent location at 15 Dr. Pilapil Street, Brgy. Sagad, Pasig City. This act of kindness has enabled Tahanan to establish roots and create a lasting impact on the lives of children in need.

The organization is registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). It is registered, licensed, and accredited by the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD).

Our Vision, Mission, and Program Objectives:

Illuminating a Path of Love and Transformation

VISION

A world where God's love and care for children.

MISSION

  • • To serve God and bring Christ to the lives of abandoned, neglected, surrendered, and foundling children.
  • • To provide a safe and loving home.
  • • To provide active advocacy on the rights of the children.
  • • To foster partnerships with churches, government, and NGOs through collaboration and networking.

GOAL

To provide a safe and secure family environment to the children to help them attain holistic life by providing shelter, spiritual formation, and psycho-social and educational services.

OBJECTIVES

  • • To provide a therapeutic and healing environment.
  • • To provide basic education and life formations and services.
  • • To provide therapeutic counseling and programs and services.

PROGRAM DESCRIPTION

At Tahanan ng Pagmamahal Children's Home, our comprehensive programs are designed to provide holistic care and development in a nurturing environment, encompassing various aspects of their well-being. We strive to ensure each child's holistic development and growth under our care through our Remedial, Health & Medical Services, Psycho-Social Services, Education, Alternative Family Care Program, Reintegration, and Spiritual Enhancement initiatives.
